I started my travel representation company RB Reps in 2007, after having worked in travel and wildlife camps for 25 years. I now have the pleasure and the privilege of living in Dartmoor National Park in Devon, UK. RB Reps represents DMC's/Ground Operators in Oman, Georgia, Jordan & UAE, Mongolia, Egypt and India and offers their services on the ground to Tour Operators in the UK, USA & Canada. All the DMC's are very well established, belong to international associations and work with extremely well known Tour Operators. The dmc's I represent, are professional and innovative and share a passion for curating excellent and unique travel experiences for clients who journey with us through these wonderful destinations. Between the 7 companies they handle FIT's, Groups, Special Interest travel, Cruise ships and Private Jets. Several of the companies are Associate Members of USTOA. Between them they attend major trade shows like WTM, ITB, ATM, USTOA, ATTA. I undertake sales trips on their behalf regularly in the UK and 4 times a year to the USA & Canada.
