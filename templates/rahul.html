I grew up in the fascinating city of Calcutta in India and schooled between the desert and mountains. After a brief stint in university I spent a year with my uncle, the famous tiger man, Billy Arjan Singh, in Dudhwa National Park, who was rehabilitating leopards and a tiger into the jungle. A 6 week visit to Tiger Tops in the Royal Chitawan National turned into 9 years working for the famous Tiger Tops group of companies and included exciting projects like building the first private jungle camp in Bandhavgarh in 1984, visiting Russia in search of tigers and working for a summer in the Amazon & Galapagos. My wonderful years in Nepal culminated in running the Tiger Tops operation as Chief Naturalist as well as serving as Dr Chuck McDougal's principal research assistant on a long term tiger monitoring project. The next chapter was 7 years criss-crossing the Soviet Union setting up the Travel Russia dmc and working for Steppes Travel in the UK. A further 7 years was spent in more mainstream travel running sales & representation offices across the world as the Director Sales for Cox & Kings. In 2007 I started RB Reps and now live in a beautiful remote National Park called Dartmoor in the depths of rural Devon in England with my lovely wife and 2 wonderful sons.
